18 Pages - Movie Review

Movie: 18 Pages

Starring: Nikhil Siddhartha, Anupama Parameswaran

Director: Palnati Surya Pratap

Release Date: 23 December 2022

Rating: ★★★★☆

Review:

"18 Pages" is a romantic drama that beautifully captures the essence of love and friendship. is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language romantic thriller film written by Sukumar and directed by Palnati Surya Pratap.Produced by GA2 Pictures and Sukumar Writings, the film stars Nikhil Siddharth and Anupama Parameswaran.

Siddhu is a phone addicted person who is an app developer and lives alone far away from his family. Siddhu's girlfriend cheats on him with another guy which leads him into a depressed state. He faces humiliation in his face due to which he attacks his cheated girlfriend's new boyfriend due to which he lands in police station.

The diary belongs to Nandini. Nandini is a strange woman who does not use a Mobile Phone, and believes in human interaction. Siddu connects with the diary and develops feelings for the woman. Nandini visited Hyderabad to deliver a letter from her grandfather to a man named Venkat Rao.

After 18 pages of reading, the diary happened to be empty. At first, Siddhu thought she stopped her dairy journalism since her task was completed.Six months later, Nandini returns to her regular life and is surprised to see all her tasks she hoped to complete before the accident accomplished by Siddu.

She ponders his identity and enquires with the bus conductor, Sandeep and Siddu’s best friend, Bhagi. She finds out that Siddu will be going to Kashi to commemorate the death anniversary of Nandini's parents. In hopes to find Siddu, she boards the same compartment as him. She found out Siddu's identity when he tells a vendor to make muntha masala in Nandini's way. She finally recognizes and reciprocates her love to Siddu.
